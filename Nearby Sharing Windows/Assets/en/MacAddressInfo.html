This feature is currently in alpha, please expect slow and unreliable transfers as well as any other kinds of bugs.
You can currently only receive one (small) file at a time and the only transport available is bluetooth.
If you can't receive data check you settings, debug log and have a look at the FAQ (in the Settings).
If you encounter an issue, please report it directly via GitHub!
https://github.com/ShortDevelopment/Nearby-Sharing-Windows/issues

Required Action


Because of privacy concerns (ads), android does not allow apps to read your bluetooth mac-address any more.
But it's required to tell the other device where to send your data (like your home address).
To proceed, please insert your Bluetooth mac address below.
You can find it in the settings at System > About > Bluetooth MAC address.
If this address is invalid, you will not be able to receive any data!

I just want to test, how other devices out there feel about my implementation so please don't feel too disappointed...
