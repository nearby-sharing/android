Welcome!

This app allows you to send and receive files and websites to and from "Windows 10 / 11" devices.

Setup - Android

  • Please enable bluetooth!
  • The location permission is required to scan for nearby devices.

Setup - Windows

  • Please follow the instructions at https://nearshare.shortdev.de/docs/setup on your Windows device.

Sending

  • You can send data by using the native android sharing menu or the button below.
  • You will see a notification sent from your Windows OS itself to accept the transfer.

Receiving

  • You can receive files by clicking the button below or using the quick tile.
